Their Story

Drew and Aurelia met in Kirksville, Missouri, when they were both attending Truman State University. They were introduced at a party in the summer of 2011 by mutual friends.

"Drew was actually dating one of my friend's friends. So I instantly put him in a box." -Aurelia

"It's funny looking back now, how much I knew I wanted to get to know her almost instantly." -Drew

As time passed, the two got to know each other as friends and a kinship grew.

"We were at this small house party one night that summer, and we must have talked on the couch for at least three hours straight. That's when I knew I had to date her." - Drew

"I actually had no idea he was interested in me at all until one night when he flirted with me in such an obvious way that it finally clicked. I was immensley flattered and thought that if he asked me out I would definately say yes." -Aurelia

Exactly seven years later, the couple got married in front of immediate family in Tower Grove Park in St. Louis, Missouri.
