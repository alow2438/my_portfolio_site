What does an MA in English, an entry level position in marketing, and learning to code have in common?

The simple answer, is me! In 2014 I recieved my Masters in English from Truman State University. Unlike my colleagues, I wanted to persue a career outside of teaching. I eventually landed an entry level job in Marketing as a Marketing Coordinator for a small consumer electronics company.

While there have certainly been aspects of Marketing that I love, I have realised that due to the nature of my small company's business model, I haven't actually gained many lucrative marketings skills. Having looked for the past year or so at marketing positions, I felt like I would end up doing the same work, which had grown tedious, for someone else and this wasn't what I wanted.

Knowing my struggle to find career satisfaction, my friend, a coder, suggested I look into LaunchCode. While I had used a small amount of HTML within my marketing role, I had never thought about doing more with it.

What is LaunchCode?

In a nutshell, LaunchCode's mission is to free offer education for individuals seeking careers in technology, or to further hone their skills for more advanced placement. Basically, everyone is welcome. To learn more about LaunchCode click here.
